# Ralph Dashboard KPI Review Cadence

## Weekly Review

BigRalph runs a weekly KPI review every Monday.
BigRalph reviews operational health, data freshness, and top failures.

Agenda
- BigRalph reviews KPI deltas and key alerts.
- BigRalph inspects data freshness lag and collector errors.
- BigRalph confirms backlog movement and blockers.
- BigRalph assigns remediation tasks with owners.

## Monthly Review

BigRalph runs a monthly KPI review on the first business day of the month.
BigRalph recalibrates thresholds, updates owners, and records decisions.

Agenda
- BigRalph reviews trendlines and SLA compliance.
- BigRalph updates metric thresholds and targets.
- BigRalph audits coverage completeness by module.
- BigRalph confirms roadmap progress and resourcing.

## Quarterly Review

BigRalph runs a quarterly review in the first week of each quarter.
BigRalph updates strategy, module scope, and investment priorities.

Agenda
- BigRalph evaluates dashboard impact and adoption.
- BigRalph adjusts module roadmap and priorities.
- BigRalph confirms ownership map and escalation paths.

## Owners

BigRalph owns KPI review execution.
ProductRalph leads own module-specific metrics.
Ralph owns platform reliability metrics and incident reviews.

## Records

BigRalph logs review outcomes in `org/decisions.md`.
